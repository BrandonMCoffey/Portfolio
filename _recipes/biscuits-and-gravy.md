ingredients:

- 1 package of pork sausage
- 1/4 cup of flour
- 2 1/2 cups of milk
- Salt and Pepper
instructions:
- Bake biscuits
- Cook sausage in a skillet until fully cooked. Stir in flour, gradually add milk and cook until it simmers and thickens into a gravy. Season with salt and pepper
